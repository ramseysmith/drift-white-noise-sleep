Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 24, 2025

Your privacy matters to us. Drift is designed with privacy at its core. We don't collect your personal information, we don't track what you listen to, and we don't sell your data. This policy explains exactly what data we do and don't collect.

1. Information We Collect

Short Answer: We collect almost nothing. Drift does not require an account, does not track your listening habits, and does not collect personal information.

1.1 Information You Provide

Drift does not require you to create an account or provide any personal information. The app works immediately after download without any registration.

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

We collect minimal technical information to operate the app:

  • App Usage Statistics: Anonymous data about crashes, app opens, and basic feature usage through Apple App Store Connect and Google Play Console analytics. This data is not linked to you personally.
  • Device Information: Basic device type and operating system version for compatibility purposes. This helps us fix bugs and optimize performance.

1.3 Sound Preferences

Your sound selections, volume level, and timer settings are stored locally on your device only. We do not have access to this information, and it never leaves your device.

2. Third-Party Services

Drift uses the following third-party services that may collect data:

2.1 Advertising (AdMob by Google)

If you are using the free version of Drift, we display advertisements through Google AdMob. AdMob may collect:

  • Device advertising ID
  • IP address
  • Device information (type, OS version)
  • Ad interaction data

This data is used to serve personalized ads. You can opt out of personalized advertising in your device settings:

  • iOS: Settings → Privacy → Advertising → Limit Ad Tracking
  • Android: Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization

Google's privacy policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

Note: Drift Premium subscribers do not see ads and are not subject to AdMob data collection.

2.2 In-App Purchases (Apple/Google)

Subscription purchases are processed by Apple (for iOS) or Google (for Android). We receive only:

  • Subscription status (active/inactive)
  • Subscription start and end dates
  • Receipt validation data

We do not receive your payment information, billing address, or personal details. All payment data is handled exclusively by Apple or Google.

  • Apple's privacy policy: https://www.apple.com/privacy/
  • Google's privacy policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

2.3 Analytics

We use standard analytics provided by Apple App Store Connect and Google Play Console to understand:

  • How many people download and use Drift
  • Which devices and OS versions people use
  • App crashes and technical errors

This data is anonymized and aggregated. It cannot be used to identify you individually.

3. How We Use Information

The limited data we collect is used solely to:

  • Operate and maintain the app
  • Process subscription purchases
  • Fix bugs and improve performance
  • Understand which features are used (anonymously)
  • Display ads (free version only)

We do NOT:

  • Sell your data to third parties
  • Track your listening habits
  • Share your information with marketers
  • Use your data for purposes unrelated to operating Drift

4. Data Storage and Security

Local Storage: All your preferences (sound selections, volume, timer settings) are stored locally on your device. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us.

Cloud Storage: We do not use cloud storage or servers to store your data. Drift operates entirely offline after initial download.

Security: While we don't collect sensitive personal data, we follow industry-standard security practices for any data we do process (like subscription validation).

5. Children's Privacy

Drift is safe for all ages, including children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children or anyone else. The app does not require registration, account creation, or any personal information.

Parents should be aware that the free version displays ads through Google AdMob. While we take steps to show age-appropriate ads, we recommend Drift Premium for children to ensure an ad-free experience.

6. Your Rights and Choices

6.1 Access and Control

Since we don't collect personal data about you, there is no personal data to access, modify, or delete. Your preferences are stored locally on your device and can be reset by:

  • Deleting and reinstalling the app
  • Using the "Reset Settings" option in the app (if available)

6.2 Opt-Out of Personalized Ads

You can opt out of personalized advertising:

  • iOS: Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking → Turn off "Allow Apps to Request to Track"
  • Android: Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization

6.3 Remove Ads Entirely

Subscribe to Drift Premium ($1.99/month) to completely remove ads and associated ad tracking.

6.4 Manage Your Subscription

  • iOS: Settings → [Your Name] → Subscriptions → Drift
  • Android: Google Play Store → Menu → Subscriptions → Drift

7. International Users

Drift is available worldwide. If you're using Drift outside the United States, please note:

  • Your local preferences are stored on your device (not transferred internationally)
  • Subscription processing is handled by Apple or Google in your region
  • AdMob may transfer data internationally according to Google's privacy policy

GDPR (European Users): You have the right to access, rectify, or erase your data. Since we don't collect personal data, these rights primarily apply to data collected by Google AdMob. Contact Google directly to exercise these rights regarding ad data.

CCPA (California Users): Drift does not sell personal information. The only data collection happens through third-party services (AdMob for ads, Apple/Google for subscriptions), which you can opt out of as described above.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make significant changes, we will:

  • Update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page
  • Notify you through the app or app store update notes
  • Obtain your consent if required by law

We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Continued use of Drift after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

9. Data Retention

Since we don't collect personal data, there's nothing to retain or delete on our end. Third-party services retain data according to their own policies:

  • AdMob: Follow Google's data retention policies
  • Apple/Google Subscriptions: Follow Apple/Google's transaction retention policies (typically for tax and legal compliance)

When you delete the Drift app, all local data (preferences, settings) is permanently deleted from your device.

10. Third-Party Links

Drift may contain links to third-party websites (like our support page or this privacy policy hosted on GitHub Pages). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these external sites. Please review their privacy policies separately.

Summary

In plain English:

Drift respects your privacy. We don't collect personal information, we don't track what sounds you listen to, and we don't sell your data. The free version shows ads through Google AdMob (which may track you for ad purposes), but you can remove ads with Drift Premium. Your preferences stay on your device. That's it.
